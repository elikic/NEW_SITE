Kinneret Innovation Center was founded on the shores of the Sea of Galilee the historic birthplace of Zionist agricultural settlement. Rooted in a legacy of local innovation, we continue the pioneering spirit of the region, leveraging 120 years of expertise and assets in advanced agriculture and water technologies. Our mission is to build a knowledge-driven tech economy in Israels north,addressing two major challenges:

Globally

developing breakthrough solutions in water, agriculture, and climate, to ensure long-term sustainability, food security, and access to clean water worldwide.

Nationally

strengthening the northern region, attracting technological talent, and creating high-quality employment opportunities.
