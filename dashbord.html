Kinneret Innovation & Science Park

Kinneret Innovation & Science Park is the first and only high- tech park in northeastern Israel. The park will cover 28,000 sqm along the Sea of Galilee. The first building in the planned Innovation Boulevard offers 7200sqm advanced infrastructure, access to cutting-edge labs, and a growing tech community. The park serves as a hub for high-tech companies, researchers, and startups, aiming to promote economic and social development and increase high- quality job opportunities in the region
