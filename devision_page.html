K-ECO

Entrepreneurship & Innovation Programs for the Next Generation.

ECO is a long-term educational program for youth leadership, technological entrepreneurship, and environmental responsibilityinitiated by KKL in collaboration with the Kinneret Innovation Center.
The program supports high school students from Israels northern periphery over five years, equipping them with the mindset and tools to think differently, take responsibility, and shape their own future.
It begins with the formation of a local leadership groupteens who develop a deep sense of belonging to their city and environment, along with values of entrepreneurship, innovation, and community commitment
and culminates in the creation of a real technological startup that addresses an environmental challenge.

Throughout the program, participants:

  • Grow personally and socially building leadership, teamwork, resilience, and self-confidence.
  •  Learn by doing working on real-world challenges through project-based learning (PBL).
  • Explore advanced technologies such as AI, engineering, and green tech, with access to innovation labs and expert guidance.
  • Build a real startup forming a team, developing a technological solution, creating a business plan, and advancing through professional mentoring.
  • Receive ongoing mentoring from industry and academic professionals who guide them along the way.
  • Present at a final showcase event a Demo Day in front of investors, researchers, industry leaders, and family.
  • And just as importantlyafter graduation, we stay connected. We continue to support our alumni through their military service, academic studies, and entry into the workforce.
